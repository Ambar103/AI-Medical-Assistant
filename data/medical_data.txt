Fever is a condition where body temperature rises above normal and may be caused by infections. Symptoms include high temperature, chills, sweating, headache, and fatigue.

Flu symptoms include fever, chills, body aches, fatigue, and cough.

COVID-19 symptoms include fever, cough, fatigue, and loss of taste or smell.

Dengue symptoms include high fever, severe headache, joint pain, nausea, and rash.

Typhoid symptoms include prolonged fever, weakness, stomach pain, and headache.

Malaria symptoms include fever, chills, sweating, headache, nausea, and vomiting.

Diabetes symptoms include increased thirst, frequent urination, fatigue, blurred vision, and weight loss.

Hypertension symptoms include headache, dizziness, chest pain, and shortness of breath.

Asthma symptoms include shortness of breath, wheezing, chest tightness, and coughing.

Tuberculosis symptoms include persistent cough, chest pain, weight loss, fever, and night sweats.

Pneumonia symptoms include cough, fever, difficulty breathing, chest pain, and fatigue.

Bronchitis symptoms include cough, mucus production, fatigue, shortness of breath, and chest discomfort.

Migraine symptoms include severe headache, nausea, vomiting, and sensitivity to light.

Common cold symptoms include runny nose, sneezing, sore throat, cough, and mild fever.

Allergy symptoms include sneezing, itching, runny nose, watery eyes, and skin rash.

Food poisoning symptoms include nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, stomach cramps, and fever.

Gastritis symptoms include stomach pain, nausea, vomiting, bloating, and indigestion.

Appendicitis symptoms include lower right abdominal pain, nausea, vomiting, fever, and loss of appetite.

Kidney stone symptoms include severe back pain, blood in urine, nausea, vomiting, and frequent urination.

Urinary tract infection symptoms include burning sensation while urinating, frequent urination, cloudy urine, and pelvic pain.

Liver disease symptoms include jaundice, fatigue, abdominal pain, swelling, and nausea.

Hepatitis symptoms include fatigue, nausea, abdominal pain, jaundice, and dark urine.

Anemia symptoms include fatigue, weakness, pale skin, dizziness, and shortness of breath.

Thyroid disorder symptoms include weight changes, fatigue, mood swings, and irregular heartbeat.

Depression symptoms include sadness, loss of interest, fatigue, sleep problems, and difficulty concentrating.

Anxiety symptoms include excessive worry, restlessness, rapid heartbeat, sweating, and difficulty concentrating.

Heart attack symptoms include chest pain, shortness of breath, sweating, nausea, and dizziness.

Stroke symptoms include sudden numbness, confusion, trouble speaking, vision problems, and severe headache.

Arthritis symptoms include joint pain, stiffness, swelling, and reduced motion.

Osteoporosis symptoms include bone pain, fractures, and loss of height.

Back pain symptoms include muscle ache, stiffness, and limited movement.

Obesity symptoms include excessive body fat, fatigue, and breathing difficulty.

Insomnia symptoms include difficulty falling asleep, waking up frequently, and daytime fatigue.

Sleep apnea symptoms include loud snoring, breathing pauses during sleep, and daytime sleepiness.

Psoriasis symptoms include red patches, itching, dry skin, and scaling.

Eczema symptoms include itchy skin, redness, swelling, and dryness.

Skin cancer symptoms include unusual growths, sores that do not heal, and changes in moles.

Breast cancer symptoms include lump in breast, changes in shape, pain, and nipple discharge.

Prostate cancer symptoms include difficulty urinating, blood in urine, and pelvic discomfort.

Colon cancer symptoms include changes in bowel habits, blood in stool, fatigue, and weight loss.

Lung cancer symptoms include persistent cough, chest pain, shortness of breath, and weight loss.

Pancreatitis symptoms include upper abdominal pain, nausea, vomiting, and fever.

Gallstones symptoms include abdominal pain, nausea, vomiting, and indigestion.

Celiac disease symptoms include diarrhea, bloating, fatigue, and weight loss.

Irritable bowel syndrome symptoms include abdominal pain, bloating, diarrhea, and constipation.

Vertigo symptoms include dizziness, spinning sensation, nausea, and balance problems.

Dehydration symptoms include thirst, dry mouth, fatigue, dizziness, and dark urine.

Heatstroke symptoms include high body temperature, confusion, nausea, and rapid heartbeat.
